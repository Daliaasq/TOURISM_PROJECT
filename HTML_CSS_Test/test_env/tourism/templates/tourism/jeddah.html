Jeddah - A Gateway to the Red Sea

Located on the western coast of Saudi Arabia, Jeddah is a vibrant and cosmopolitan city that offers a unique blend of old-world charm and modern amenities. Known as the gateway to the Red Sea, Jeddah is a hub for water-based activities such as diving, snorkeling, and fishing. The city is also home to a rich cultural heritage, with historic sites such as the Al-Balad district, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the Jeddah Corniche, a popular waterfront promenade. Jeddah is also a foodie's paradise, with a wide range of international and local cuisines to tantalize your taste buds. From the bustling souks to the modern malls, Jeddah is a city that has something for everyone.

Activities

Jeddah is a city that offers a wide range of activities and experiences for visitors to enjoy. From exploring historic sites to indulging in water-based activities, there is never a dull moment in Jeddah.

Snorkeling and Diving

Explore the stunning underwater world of the Red Sea with a snorkeling or diving excursion in Jeddah. The crystal-clear waters are home to a diverse range of marine life and coral reefs, making it a prime destination for underwater exploration.

Historic Sites

Discover Jeddah's rich cultural heritage with a visit to its manyhistoric sites. Explore the Al-Balad district, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and wander through its narrow streets lined with beautiful old houses. Visit the Jeddah Corniche, a popular waterfront promenade that offers stunning views of the Red Sea and the city's skyline.

Fishing

Jeddah is a popular destination for fishing enthusiasts, with a wide range of fishing charters and tours available. Whether you're a seasoned angler or a beginner, Jeddah offers a variety of fishing experiences for all levels.
